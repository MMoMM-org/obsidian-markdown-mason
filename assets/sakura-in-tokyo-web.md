# Question

when will sakura be in tokyo next year

## Answer

In Tokyo next year (2026), sakura are forecast to start blooming around March 19–20 and reach their best viewing period roughly March 26–April 4. [thestar.com](https://www.thestar.com.my/aseanplus/aseanplus-news/2025/12/18/japan-releases-first-sakura-forecast-for-2026-tokyo-to-see-blooms-from-mid-march)

## Key dates for Tokyo 2026

- First blossoms (kaika): Around March 19–20 in central Tokyo. [n-kishou](https://n-kishou.com/corp/news-contents/sakura/?lang=en)
- Full bloom (mankai): Around March 26–28. [timeout](https://www.timeout.com/asia/news/the-2026-japan-cherry-blossom-forecast-is-out-heres-when-to-catch-sakura-in-full-bloom-010326)
- “Best viewing” window: Commonly given as about March 28–April 4 for Tokyo. [japan-guide](https://www.japan-guide.com/sakura/)

So if you aim your trip for roughly March 27–April 2, you maximize the probability of catching peak hanami in the city, assuming a typical weather year. [timeout](https://www.timeout.com/tokyo/news/heres-the-first-official-japan-cherry-blossom-forecast-for-2026-121925)

## Practical planning tip

Forecasts can still shift by several days due to warm/cold spells, so if you are locking in flights early, consider a 7–10 day window centered on March 27 to hedge against variation. [tokyocheapo](https://tokyocheapo.com/living/tokyo-cherry-blossom-forecast/)

# Question

when will it be in Sapporo?

## Answer

In Sapporo in 2026, sakura are expected roughly from late April, with peak around the last week of April. [n-kishou](https://n-kishou.com/corp/news-contents/sakura/?lang=en)
## Key dates for Sapporo 2026
- First blossoms: Forecasts cluster around April 18–25 for first bloom. [n-kishou](https://n-kishou.com/corp/news-contents/sakura/?lang=en)
- Full bloom: Around April 24–May 1, depending on the forecaster. [japan](https://www.japan.travel/en/see-and-do/cherry-blossom-forecast/)
- Best viewing window: A safe bet is roughly April 24–30 for Sapporo city parks such as Maruyama Park and Hokkaido Shrine. [timeout](https://www.timeout.com/asia/news/the-2026-japan-cherry-blossom-forecast-is-out-heres-when-to-catch-sakura-in-full-bloom-010326)

So if you’re aligning this with Tokyo, think Tokyo around March 27–April 2, then Sapporo about three to four weeks later, around April 24–30. [japan-guide](https://www.japan-guide.com/sakura/)
